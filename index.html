Where nature meets art

We invite you to explore Indonesia’s rich geological heritage through an immersive and interactive experience that blends scientific discovery with artistic expression. BUMI is a space where curiosity is sparked, stories of nature unfold, and inspiration thrives.

The Surface

The BUMI Museum was founded to bridge the gap between science, art, and cultural heritage, offering visitors a deeper understanding of the geological history of Indonesia. BUMI Museum seeks to inspire a sense of wonder and responsibility toward preserving the earth’s geological treasures for future generations.
