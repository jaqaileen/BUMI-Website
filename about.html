Vision Statement

To inspire curiosity and foster a deep appreciation for Earth’s geological wonders, empowering all generations with knowledge of our planet’s dynamic history, resources, and future.


Mission Statement

To honor Indonesia’s geological legacy through immersive exhibitions that blend cultural and scientific storytelling, fostering community connections to the natural world

BUMI Museum's Commitment to Education and Natural History

At BUMI Museum, our passion for natural history drives us to inspire and educate the next generation of explorers. Through immersive exhibits and interactive experiences, we bring the wonders of geology, minerals, and igneous formations to life, fostering a deeper understanding of Earth's dynamic processes. Our mission is to ignite curiosity and foster a lifelong love for science, connecting visitors to the natural world in ways that spark creativity, inspire learning, and cultivate environmental stewardship for the future.
